Thanks for being here!

My name is Emily May, and I'm running for Fargo Parks & Recreation Board because I want to contribute my professional experience, passion for community service, and love for outdoors to the continued success of our community. The Fargo Parks & Recreation provides immense value to the health and wellbeing of our citizens and contributes to the foundation for meaningful economic community development, making Fargo an attractive place to live and hard place to leave!

Having grown up in Fargo, my connection and commitment to this community run deep. I know firsthand the values, spirit, and hard work that make this city exceptional. Fargo shaped me, and now I'm ready to give back. I aim to strengthen social cohesion and elevate transparency in local government decision-making. As a responsible steward of our tax payers’ dollars, I will ensure the Fargo Parks policies and projects benefit the entire community.

I am committed to:

1) Developing a deep understanding of community needs and priorities

2) Increasing citizen involvement in local government decision-making by removing barriers to engagement and strengthening private-public relationships

3) Making decisions based on community-derived insights rather than distant, partisan mandates or personal agendas

Let’s build a strong, transparent, community minded future for Fargo — together.
